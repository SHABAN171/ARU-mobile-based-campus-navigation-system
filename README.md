The Lifecycle Cost Analysis and Capital Portfolio Project Management System (LCCA-CPPM System) is a web-based application designed to assist organizations in evaluating, managing, and optimizing capital investment projects throughout their lifecycle.

The system integrates Lifecycle Cost Analysis (LCCA) techniques with Capital Project Portfolio Management (CPPM) to support strategic decision-making, financial planning, and resource allocation.

It enables institutions to analyze total ownership costs, prioritize projects, manage budgets, and monitor performance efficiently.

To calculate and analyze total lifecycle costs of capital projects.

To assist in selecting and prioritizing projects within a portfolio.

To improve transparency in budgeting and financial forecasting.

To enhance monitoring and control of capital investments.

To support data-driven decision-making.
